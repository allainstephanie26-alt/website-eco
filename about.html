Our Story

Preserving Batanes through sustainable tourism

The EcoJourney Mission

EcoJourney was born from a deep love for Batanes and a commitment to protect its extraordinary beauty. What started as a small group of passionate locals and environmentalists has grown into a movement dedicated to sustainable tourism.

In 2024, we witnessed the increasing impact of tourism on our fragile island ecosystem. We realized that without intervention, the very beauty that attracts visitors could be lost forever. This urgency drove us to create EcoJourney.

Our platform connects travelers with authentic, sustainable experiences while educating them about environmental stewardship. We work directly with local communities, ensuring tourism benefits residents and preserves Ivatan culture.

Through partnerships with conservation groups, local businesses, and government agencies, we've established guidelines for responsible travel. Every visitor who chooses EcoJourney contributes to protecting Batanes for future generations.

Today, we're proud to have helped thousands of travelers experience Batanes sustainably, while supporting local livelihoods and conservation efforts. But our journey has just begun.
