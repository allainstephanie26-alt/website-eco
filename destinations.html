Hidden Gem

Marlboro Hills

Rolling green hills in Racuh a Payaman that resemble Marlboro Country advertisements. Best visited at sunrise for stunning golden-hour photography.

Hidden Gem

Valugan Boulder Beach

Unique beach covered with smooth andesite boulders formed by Mt. Iraya's eruption. Located in Basco, perfect for dramatic coastal photography.

Cultural

Chavayan Village

Traditional Ivatan stone houses in Sabtang Island. A living museum showcasing centuries-old Ivatan architecture and culture.

Cultural

Basco Lighthouse (Naidi Hills)

Historic white lighthouse built in 2003 offering 360-degree views of Basco town, Mt. Iraya, and the West Philippine Sea.

Stay

Fundacion Pacita

Eco-friendly boutique lodge in Basco with traditional stone walls, stunning views, and sustainable practices honoring local culture.

Stay

Ivatan Homestays

Authentic cultural immersion staying with local families in traditional stone houses. Experience genuine Ivatan hospitality and lifestyle.

Go-To

Racuh a Payaman (Marlboro Country)

Vast pastureland with grazing cattle and dramatic coastal cliffs. One of the most photographed spots in Batanes.

Go-To

Vayang Rolling Hills

Picturesque green hills in Basco perfect for hiking, picnics, and watching the sunrise. Offers panoramic island views.

Cultural

Honesty Coffee Shop

Famous unmanned store where customers pay on honor system. Reflects the remarkable honesty and trust in Batanes culture.

Hidden Gem

Alapad Rock Formation

Natural rock arch formation in Mahatao created by centuries of wave erosion. Spectacular for photography during low tide.

Go-To

Sabtang Island

Island accessible by falowa (traditional boat). Home to traditional villages, pristine beaches, and preserved Ivatan culture.

Hidden Gem

Chamantad-Tinyan Viewpoint

Breathtaking cliff-top viewpoint in Uyugan offering views of lush valleys, traditional villages, and the Pacific Ocean.

Cultural

San Carlos Borromeo Church

Historic coral stone church in Mahatao built in 1787. One of the oldest and most beautiful churches in Batanes.

Go-To

Homoron Blue Lagoon

Crystal-clear natural pool in Sabtang Island surrounded by dramatic rock formations. Perfect for swimming during calm weather.

Hidden Gem

Mount Iraya

Active stratovolcano and highest peak in Batanes (1,009m). Challenging but rewarding climb with permits required from local government.
